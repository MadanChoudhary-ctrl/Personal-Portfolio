Effect of Technology on Human Life

With increasing proliferation, technology continues to be an important part of our lives. There are positive as well as negative impacts of technology on our daily lives.

Positive Impact of Technology

Every individual are dependent on technology for every minuscule operation. Technology has taken communication to a new level where people can have face to face conversation over internet. It has made student's life ease with the development of online schools, e-learning programs, online books, etc. With the emerging innovation of technology like transportation, it has made the movement from place to place so easy and cheap. Technologies such as computers, smartphones have made it easier for people of today to exchange information, make faster decisions, interact socially, get entertainment, process financial transactions efficiently, do online transaction, etc.


Negative Impact of Technology

New Technology Improves quality of life on one hand and on the other hand it deteriorates the environment. Nowadays, people are bacoming so lazy because of the heavy usage of technology. Many people are facing eye problem, headache due to the use of mobile. With the increase in industrialization, the fresh air which we breathe is being polluted. In addition to making our lives more convenient enhancing communication, smartphones and computers are seemed to be addicting and can cause insomnia, mental disorder, loss of eye-sight, and so forth.
